Nick Hughes

  • Doctorate with a specialty in sports medicine and outpatient orthopedics, University of Buffalo

    • Nick is from Buffalo, NY and attended Fordham University. After an injury during a lacrosse game, he became involved in physical therapy. Nick graduated with a Doctorate from the University of Buffalo in 2008 with a specialty in sports medicine and outpatient orthopedics. With a strong manual therapy background, patients receive a combination of soft tissue mobilization, massage and exercise to relieve pain and increase daily function. Nick works closely with a pain management doctor to get you quickly back to a pain-free lifestyle.
