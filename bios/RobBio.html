Rob Lovell

  • Bachelor of Science & Chemistry minor, University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill
  • Master of Science in Physical Therapy, University Of Miami School Of Medicine

    • Rob graduated on the Dean’s List from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill with a Bachelor of Science degree and Chemistry minor. He completed his graduate studies with honors at the University Of Miami School Of Medicine with a Master of Science degree in Physical Therapy. He graduated with a 3.9 GPA on a 4.0 scale. Rob has practiced physical therapy for over twelve years. He has worked exclusively in private practice and with adults with orthopedic and neurological conditions. Rob and his partners opened up Fitness Quest Punta Gorda in May 2004 to provide the highest quality physical therapy services for the residents of Charlotte County.

    He has extensive experience in the physical therapy evaluation and treatment of patients with Spine, Shoulder, Elbow, Wrist/Hand, Balance/Vestibular, Neurological, Hip, Knee, and Foot/Ankle impairments. Rob has contributed to the professional physical therapy literature. He has published research on muscle fatigue and range of motion measurement and was awarded the designation of Certified Clinical Instructor by the Florida Consortium of Clinical Educators in June of 2006. A strong believer in clinical education, Rob has attended multiple continuing education courses on the spine, shoulder, knee, geriatric orthopedics, ergonomics, and balance/fall prevention.
