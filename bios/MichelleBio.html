Michelle Harmon

  • Bachelor’s degree in Exercise and Sports Science, Texas State University
  • Doctorate in Physical Therapy (DPT), University of South Florida

    • Michelle Harmon, DPT is the Clinical Director for Fitness Quest Physical Therapy in Venice, Florida. Michelle was born and raised in Houston, Texas. She attended Texas State University and received her bachelor’s degree in Exercise and Sports Science in 2006. From there she moved to Florida and began training for her Doctoral degree in Physical Therapy at the University of South Florida. In 2009 she earned her Doctorate in Physical Therapy and moved to Sarasota to begin her career. Michelle specializes in orthopedic, sports and spine physical therapy, as well as vestibular rehabilitation. Her treatments emphasize the integration of manual techniques and therapeutic exercise in orthopedic rehabilitation.

    Michelle is a member of the American Physical Therapy Association (APTA) and CPR certified. Michelle is committed to enhancing her skills as a physical therapist by continuing her education with courses in manual therapy, spinal disorders, vestibular rehabilitation, and foot and ankle disorders. Michelle has developed an environment where patients can receive one on one care, regain functional mobility, and return to a healthier lifestyle. In her spare time she enjoys spending time with family and friends, participating in exercise and recreational activities, and traveling.
